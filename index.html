About

Over the past few years, I've worked at different start-ups, starting in customer support and moving into an operations focused role. I had the opportunity to work closely with engineering teams where I helped define product issues and tested and collected user feedback. I became very curious in how the engineering teams would come up solutions and this ultimately inspired me to learn how to code.

In 2018 I enrolled in an online apprenticeship with Bloc.io and balanced my learning with working full-time. After about 1 year, I still wanted to improve my learning and took additional online courses such as - The Complete JavaScript Course 2020: Build Real Projects!, Modern React with Redux, React Front to Back.

I'm excited to use my knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and Redux to build, solve problems and create experiences through code.

Made with React to demonstrate the use of the useState() hook and ability to fetch data from an API. The app displays an icon that changes based on the weather condition. In order to display the appropriate icon, I impletented a switch statement in my Icon component.
